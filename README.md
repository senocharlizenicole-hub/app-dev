# My Favorite Series: Gilmore Girls

![Gilmore Girls Poster](https://www.businessinsider.com/gilmore-girls-friends-with-my-kid-like-lorelai-2024-12)

## Overview
**Gilmore Girls** is a heartwarming dramedy series that follows the lives of **Lorelai** and **Rory Gilmore**, a mother-daughter duo living in the quirky town of **Stars Hollow**.  
It explores family bonds, friendship, love, and the ups and downs of daily life.

## Why I Love It
- Witty and fast-paced dialogue  
- Strong mother-daughter relationship  
- Charming small-town setting  
- Memorable pop culture references  

> “Life’s short. Talk fast.” — Lorelai Gilmore

## Main Characters
1. Lorelai Gilmore  
2. Rory Gilmore  
3. Tristin Dugray
4. Luke Danes  
5. Logan Huntzberger  

## My Favorite Moment
The **Stars Hollow Festival scenes** — always full of quirky characters, laughs, and heartwarming moments.
